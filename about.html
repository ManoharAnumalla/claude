About SV Hospital

Two decades of compassionate healthcare, excellence in medicine, and unwavering commitment to our community.

Our Story

Healing Lives Since 2006

SV Hospital was founded in 2006 with a single mission: to bring world-class healthcare to the doorstep of every family in Hyderabad. What started as a small 30-bed facility in Rampally has grown into a multi-speciality hospital trusted by over 10,000 patients every year.

Our growth has been driven by our commitment to medical excellence, investment in the latest technology, and most importantly — the compassionate care provided by our team of dedicated doctors, nurses, and support staff.

Today, we stand as one of Hyderabad's most trusted healthcare institutions, offering over 9 specialities and 24/7 emergency services, all under one roof.

Our Pillars

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and exceptional healthcare services to every individual, guided by integrity, innovation, and compassion.

Our Vision

To be Hyderabad's most trusted multi-speciality hospital, setting new benchmarks in clinical outcomes, patient experience, and community health impact.

Our Values

Compassion, clinical excellence, transparency, patient dignity, and continuous learning form the core of everything we do at SV Hospital.

Our Journey

Milestones Over the Years

2006 – Foundation

SV Hospital was established in Rampally, Hyderabad with 30 beds and a dedicated team of 5 doctors, serving the local community.

2010 – First Expansion

Expanded to 80 beds and added Cardiology and Orthopedics departments. Installed our first MRI machine for advanced diagnostics.

2014 – NABH Accreditation

Received National Accreditation Board for Hospitals (NABH) certification, recognizing our commitment to quality and patient safety standards.

2018 – New ICU & Trauma Centre

Launched a 20-bed ICU and 24/7 Trauma Centre, significantly upgrading our emergency care capacity and critical care capabilities.

2022 – Robotic Surgery Wing

Introduced robotic-assisted surgery, making SV Hospital one of the few hospitals in this region to offer minimally invasive precision surgery.

2026 – 10,000+ Patients Milestone

Crossed the milestone of serving 10,000+ patients annually, with 15+ specialists and a vision to expand to a 200-bed capacity by 2028.
