Our Medical Services

Comprehensive, expert healthcare across all specialties — delivered with compassion and cutting-edge technology.

All Specialties

What We Treat

Cardiology

Full cardiac care services including non-invasive diagnostics, catheterization lab, angioplasty, pacemaker implantation, and heart surgery.

  • ECG & Echocardiography
  • Coronary Angioplasty
  • Cardiac Surgery
  • Heart Failure Management

Orthopedics

Expert bone, joint, and muscle care. From sports injuries to complex reconstructive surgeries, our orthopedic team delivers precision outcomes.

  • Knee & Hip Replacement
  • Spine Surgery
  • Sports Medicine
  • Fracture & Trauma Care

General Medicine

Comprehensive internal medicine covering infections, metabolic disorders, chronic diseases, and preventive care for adults.

  • Diabetes Management
  • Hypertension Control
  • Respiratory Conditions
  • Preventive Health Check-ups

Pediatrics

Comprehensive child health services in a safe, friendly environment — from newborn care to adolescent health management.

  • Neonatal & NICU Care
  • Vaccination Programs
  • Growth & Development
  • Pediatric Surgery

Emergency Care

24/7 emergency department with fully-equipped trauma bays, resuscitation units, and rapid-response medical teams always on standby.

  • 24/7 Trauma Centre
  • ICU & Critical Care
  • Ambulance Services
  • Rapid Response Team

Diagnostics

State-of-the-art diagnostic centre offering fast and accurate imaging, lab work, and pathology for timely clinical decisions.

  • MRI & CT Scan
  • Digital X-Ray & Ultrasound
  • Full Blood Lab Work
  • Home Sample Collection

Neurology

Expert neurological care for brain, spinal cord, and nervous system disorders with advanced neuro-diagnostics and surgical capabilities.

  • Stroke Management
  • Epilepsy Treatment
  • EEG & EMG
  • Migraine Management

Gynecology & Obstetrics

Complete women's healthcare from routine check-ups and antenatal care to complex gynecological surgeries in a caring environment.

  • Antenatal & Postnatal
  • Normal & C-Section Delivery
  • Laparoscopic Surgery
  • Fertility Consultations

Ophthalmology

Comprehensive eye care including refractive error correction, cataract surgery, retinal treatment, and glaucoma management.

  • Cataract Surgery
  • LASIK / Refractive Surgery
  • Glaucoma Treatment
  • Retinal Care
